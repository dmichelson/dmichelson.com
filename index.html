Bio

    Welcome to my web home! I'm an MD-PhD student at Harvard and MIT. I'm studying for my MD through the Heath Sciences and Technology program, and I'm doing my PhD in the lab of Diane Mathis and Christophe Benoist. Broadly speaking, I'm interested in the intersection of immunology, genomics and medicine. In my graduate work, I'm studying the mechanisms that develop and maintain immune tolerance to self, and how breakdown of these mechanisms can lead to immunodeficiency and autoimmunity.
